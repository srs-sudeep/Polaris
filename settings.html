How to Use

1. Select any Japanese text anywhere on your screen

2. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + T

3. The translation will appear in a floating overlay!

The app will automatically copy your selection when you press the hotkey.

Keyboard Shortcut

Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + T - Translate selected text

If the hotkey doesn't work:

  • Check console for registration status
  • Another app may be using this shortcut
  • Try restarting the application
  • On Windows, you may need to run as administrator
